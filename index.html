Old Dim Master


Type | Installation, game
Tools | Processing, ReacTIVision, Makey-Makey
Roles | Group work. Game design, coding

Old Dim Master is an installation game which aims to enhance the new generation and foreigners’ understanding of Hong Kong’s traditional dim sum culture. It provides simulations of making three old style Chinese dim sum dishes. Players can follow the tutorials and prepare the dim sum dishes steps by steps, as well as learning the history of the dishes after the simulation.

Mulaxy


Type | Game
Tools | Processing
Roles | Group work. Game design, coding

Mulaxy is a music game to promote that music has no limits. This is the reason why the name of the game is combined by the two word “music” and “galaxy”. Players can create their own beats or songs freely in the game. There are five planets to represent five different musical instruments. When the planet hits the earth, it will play a music note.

Color Prism


Type | Game
Tools | p5.js
Roles | Group work. Game design, coding

Color Prism is a puzzle game about color and pattern. The game objective is to place the correct color of dot in the target position in limited steps. Players need to follow a special logic of color and position reflection pattern. There are five stages in total. In the harder stages, some special items will appear to increase the difficulty of gameplay.

RYB


Type | Game
Tools | C++, SDL
Roles | Individual work. Game design, graphic, coding

RYB is a puzzle game about color and Stroop effect. For example, people find it difficult to identify the word “green” if the word is printed in red. This game is similar to the traditional tile-matching game which player manipulates tiles according to match the target colour and make them disappear. There are normal mode and crazy mode in the game.

Bo Ya & Zi Qi


Type | Game
Tools | Kinect, C#
Roles | Group work. Game design, coding

Bo Ya & Zi Qi is a Kinect based charades game for two players. It provides an environment for two players to play word guessing game using the gesture recognition and voice control technology. A player needs to act out the word or phrase while another player needs to guess and say out loud the answer. There are four questions in total. They will get better results if they can get more correct answers in a shorter period of time.

HERD


** Work In Progress **
Type | Game
Tools | Unity
Roles | Individual work. Game design, graphic, coding

HERD is a 4 players action game. The theme is about sheep herding. Players become the herding dog and gathering the sheep back to the home. To get higher mark, players need to gather more sheep and dog bones. 2 players are in a team and connected with a certain distance, so that they need to move together. Players can choose to work as a team to gather sheep or work individually to eat the bones. Eating some magic bottles can help to lengthen the player distance. Player who has the highest mark wins the game.

Coffee Maker


Type | Game
Tools | Android Studio, Java
Roles | Individual work. Game design, graphic, coding

Coffee Maker is a mobile game app of simulating coffee making. They can tilt the phone to simulate the gesture of pouring drinks. The objective is to make more cups of coffee as much as possible in the limited time. When the time is up, marks are calculated according to the number of cups the player has made and the correctness of the coffee making process.

Tea Tee


Type | Wearable
Tools | LilyPad
Roles | Group work. Idea, fabrication, coding

Tea Tee is a wearable to promote Chinese tea culture. This work aims to encourage people to drink tea in a correct and healthy way. This T-shirt is dyed with tea in the shapes of organs. The organs are correlated with the tea’s function. In the front of the T-shirt, there are six pockets carrying six different scrolls about the characteristics of the six types of tea. In the back, there are six books to tell the function, targets and principle of that tea type.
